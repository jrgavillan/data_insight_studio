# Privacy Policy - Data Insight Studio

**Last Updated:** January 2026

---

## 1. Data We Collect

When you use Data Insight Studio, we collect:

- **Homework problems** (text you type or images you upload)
- **Excel/CSV files** you upload for analysis
- **Category and difficulty** selections
- **Session information** (login credentials)
- **Usage data** (timestamps, API calls, calculations)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

### ✅ What We DO:

- Process your uploads to provide homework solutions
- Analyze your data to generate step-by-step explanations
- Generate visualizations and statistical calculations
- Track API usage for billing purposes
- Improve our service based on error reports

### ❌ What We DO NOT:

- **Store** your homework problems or uploaded files after processing
- **Train** our AI models using your data
- **Share** your data with third parties
- **Sell** your data
- **Retain** your uploads beyond the session
- **Use** your data for marketing

---

## 3. Data Retention

All uploaded files and problems are:

- **Processed immediately** when you submit
- **Deleted after** the response is generated
- **Not stored** on our servers
- **Processed and deleted** within 24 hours maximum

Session data (API logs) are retained for:
- **90 days** for billing and analytics
- **Then permanently deleted**

We are committed to educational use:

- All solutions show **step-by-step reasoning**

- We provide **learning mode** for self-guided learning
